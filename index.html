ABOUT CATECOIN

Rise of Cats is a random tower defense game that is fast and strategic. The game adopts a fast-paced design, each game can be completed in 3 minutes, allowing you to start the game at any time and enjoy the fun of cat game. Rise of Cats is an NFT game, you can freely dispose of your cat’s assets, collect, train or trade them

Collect over 40 kinds of cute cats, use their special skills to fight off your opponents or co-op with friends to defeat enemies! Rise of Cats is a cat themed fast paced random TD game, each game only takes 3 minutes! Enjoy the fun and aggressive cat arena!
